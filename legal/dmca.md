CB Studios respects intellectual property rights and expects customers, users, resellers, community members, and third parties to do the same.

This DMCA Policy describes how copyright owners may report infringement involving CB Studios platforms or materials, and how CB Studios may respond to notices involving its own copyrighted works.

CB Studios may review reported materials and take appropriate action based on the information provided, applicable law, platform rules, and available evidence.

Possible actions include:

| Action | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Request more information | CB Studios may ask for missing or unclear details |
| Remove or disable access | Content may be removed or restricted when appropriate |
| Notify affected users | Relevant users may be informed of the complaint |
| Preserve records | Evidence may be retained for legal or platform purposes |

## CB Studios Copyright Enforcement

CB Studios may submit DMCA takedown notices or platform reports when its resources are leaked, resold, uploaded, mirrored, published, copied, or distributed without authorization.

This includes unauthorized publication of:

- Scripts and source code.
- Escrowed or protected resources.
- Vehicle packs and model files.
- Audio packs and sound files.
- MLO / mapping resources.
- UI assets, logos, icons, branding, images, or documentation.
- License keys, download links, private files, or customer-only content.

## Counter Notification Process

If your content was removed or disabled because of a copyright complaint and you believe the removal was a mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter notification.

A counter notification should include:

- Your full legal name and contact information.
- Identification of the material that was removed or disabled.
- The previous location of the material before removal.
- A statement, under penalty of perjury, that you have a good-faith belief the material was removed or disabled because of mistake or misidentification.
- A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the appropriate court for your address or, if outside the United States, an appropriate jurisdiction for the service provider.
- A statement that you will accept service of process from the complaining party or its agent.
- Your physical or electronic signature.

CB Studios may forward valid counter notifications to the original complaining party or relevant platform as required by applicable procedures.

## Repeat Infringers

CB Studios may suspend or terminate access for users who repeatedly infringe copyrights, distribute unauthorized copies, submit abusive notices, or violate CB Studios license terms.
